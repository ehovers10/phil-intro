Is the world really as you think it is?

There are lots of things we don't know:

  • I don't know what lies beyond the grave.
  • I don't know what will come of the world as a result of fossil fuel burning.
  • I don't know where I left my copy of Knowledge and its Limits.

But there are also many things we are perfectly confident in:

  • I am on the Montclair State University campus.
  • I am discussing philosophy in my Intro class.
  • I had a yummy salad for lunch.

But how do we actually...

...know that the quotidian parts of the world are as they seem to be?
