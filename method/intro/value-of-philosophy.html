How does philosophy improve our understanding?

Option 1: Art as a model

  • Art can be a source of profound insight. Individuals can access the sublime through engaging with art.
  • An artist can improve understanding through metaphor and imagery.

Application to philosophy

  • Good philosophy can also be a source of profound insight.
  • But artistic insight is that it is often very fleeting and personal experience.
  • Philosophy aims to provide more lasting and objective understanding.

Option 2: Science as a model

  • Science improves our understanding of the world by discovering new facts about the way the world works.
  • When the scientist's work is accepted, we have a broader understanding of the world.

Application to philosophy

  • But this method of progress doesn't work for philosophy.
  • Philosophy rarely results in one set of facts being universally accepted.
  • We will discuss many debates that originated long ago but are still alive today.

Option 3: Depth of understanding

  • But philosophy can make progress! It does so by:
  • challenging preconceived notions about the way the world works,
  • uncovering nuance in our concepts of the world, and
  • requiring us to defend our beliefs.
